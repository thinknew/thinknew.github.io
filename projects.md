## Selected Ongoing Projects

### 1. **Neuroadaptive BCI Applications**  
Transforming brain-computer interfaces (BCI) into powerful tools for enhancing communication and cognition:
- **Cognitive Nudging**: Helping mitigate confirmation bias and improve decision-making.  
- **Collaborative Robotics**: Seamlessly integrating humans and robots in shared tasks.  
- **Spatial Learning & Awareness**: Empowering users with enhanced navigation and spatial cognition.  

These works are supported by multiple internal and external competitive grants.

---

### 2. **[Sensory Augmentation](https://sites.google.com/view/masa-dec/)**  
Revolutionizing mobility for visually impaired individuals by enhancing spatial awareness through sensory augmentation:
- **Goal**: Enable fluent and independent navigation for blind individuals.  
- **Funding**: Supported by the prestigious **NHMRC Ideas Grant**.  
- **Impact**: Developing innovative technologies that reshape assistive mobility tools.

---

### 3. **[The UTSxDream Recording](https://youtu.be/djmMWSQPfJc?si=dyUjYGqcWiIxqnqR)** (formerly DreamMachine)  
Exploring the future of image and dream reconstruction and interpretation using brain signals:
- **Objective**: Decode and reconstruct dreams from neural activity.  
- **Methods**: Advanced EEG decoding and machine learning to interpret subconscious patterns.  
- **Applications**: Insights into memory consolidation, subconscious processes, and potential therapeutic uses.
